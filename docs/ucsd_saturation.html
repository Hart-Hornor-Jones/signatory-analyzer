UC San Diego — Open-Letter Signing Saturation

The share of each department's core faculty (Senate ladder + teaching professors + emeriti) who signed the May 2026 open letter. Toggle between individual departments and broad fields.

How to read this. Saturation = signers ÷ core faculty. Hover any bar for exact counts.
  • Faculty headcounts come from the UC San Diego catalog (academic departments) and School of Biological Sciences section pages, counted by rank.
  • School denominators are a full census — every department in each field is counted, including those with zero signers (History, Literature, Cognitive Science, Anthropology, …), so the school rates are legitimate.
  • Joint-appointment signers are counted once, in their home (first-named) department, to match the denominators.
  • Global Policy & Strategy is measured against its Senate ladder only (11), so its rate runs high.
  • The Biological sciences school denominator is the de-duplicated School umbrella (180); the four section bars overlap in membership.
  • Data Science (HDSI) is a cross-appointed institute (shown as a department, excluded from school totals). School-of-Medicine signers fall outside the school measure. Theatre & Dance (0 signers) is omitted from the humanities denominator.
